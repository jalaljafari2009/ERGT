# ERGT Research Article

This folder contains the article manuscript artifacts for the ERGT research
project.

## Files

- `ergt_article_with_references_author_corrected.tex`: LaTeX source of the
  author-corrected article manuscript.
- `ergt_article_with_references_author_corrected.pdf`: compiled PDF version of
  the same manuscript.

## Role in the Project

This folder is a research manuscript archive, not an execution artifact.

The manuscript records the broader paper-facing presentation of ERGT. The
operational implementation path remains governed by
`../17_physics_aligned_ergt_program.md`, and the concise reader-facing position
paper remains `../18_ergt_position_paper.md`.

Experimental claims in the article should be kept synchronized with the
corresponding evidence reports, notebook bundles, and gate decisions in the
repository.

## Maintenance Notes

- Keep the `.tex` source and `.pdf` build in sync when the article is revised.
- Do not use the article itself as a substitute for runnable configs,
  measurement contracts, or evidence reports.
- If a claim changes because of new experiments, update the article and the
  relevant design document together.
